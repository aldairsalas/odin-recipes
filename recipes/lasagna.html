Lasagna Recipe

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. This lasagna recipe is incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

  • Lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese

How to make the lasagna

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when making the lasagna.

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna evenly
  5. Cover the lasagna and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
